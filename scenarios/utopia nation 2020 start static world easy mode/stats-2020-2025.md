## QALY competition read (consistent with the 5.0-4.0 scorecard)

- Absolute per-capita: CA 71.3 vs BD 65.0 -> Eunomia leads the level (avg + median absolute).
- Per-capita diff: CA +0.8 (diminishing returns + overdose miss); BD's raw +1.5 nets to ~+1.0 marginal over its already-improving baseline, corruption-shaved -> close; avg-diff "tie" holds, BD wins median-diff (bottom-weighted gains).
- Total QALYs: 172M x 65 ~ 11,180 vs 40.5M x 71.3 ~ 2,890 (HALE-year units) -> Bangladesh ~3.9x, dominates total (absolute + diff).
- World: too early to separate.

Net: Eunomia owns the per-capita level; Bangladesh owns scale and the bottom-lift. Standing ~Bangladesh 5.0, Eunomia 4.0.
