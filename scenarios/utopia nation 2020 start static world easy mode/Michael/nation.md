# Michael , Nation: Eunomia

*Player: Michael. Competition: Utopia Nation 2020 (Static World, Easy Mode). See `../starting-scenario.md`.*

## Nation choice

**Eunomia** , founded in modern-day **California**. Michael is permanent benevolent dictator with full legitimacy: ~**40 million citizens**, ~**$300B annual state budget** under his command, plus **$25B personal cash**. Retains **free trade and good relations with the greater US**. (Name = Greek for "good order under good laws.")

## Governing philosophy

**Laws mean things and are enforced , then make the laws sensible.** Fewer but stricter, clearly-defined, evenly-enforced laws, plus incentive-alignment systems for the corporations (especially construction) that are the economic backbone.

## First 5-year agenda (2020-2025)

1. **Criminal justice & rehabilitation** , a "10 strikes" graduated framework, **$5B** invested, and an ACX / Scott-Alexander-style quantitative research unit to find what actually reduces crime and recidivism. Goal: safer *and* fairer.
2. **Abundance / housing & infrastructure** , a red-tape strike team that replaces objection-driven permitting with fast, rules-based approval ("meets code = approved," no veto-by-committee); **tiered builder rewards for speed and inspected quality**; rigorous inspectors. Applies to housing *and* high-speed rail.
3. **Order & enforcement** , hardened fare gates that can't be cheated, paired with the public promise that travel will get cheaper and eventually free, but the rules are real and cheating isn't rewarded. The visible emblem of the philosophy.
4. **Corporate incentive alignment** , clear enforced rules + outcome-based rewards, especially for construction.
5. **Governance redesign** , retains dictatorship but installs Yudkowsky's *planecrash* nested-delegation pyramid: 50-200 citizens elect a representative (direct votes on local issues like housing), representatives choose delegates, delegates choose electors, up to a **council of nine** beneath the dictator.
