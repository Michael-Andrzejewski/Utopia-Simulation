# Eunomia , Canon Timeline

*Player: Michael. The canonical, adjudicated history of Eunomia, 2020-2070. Optimistic/pessimistic explorations live in `history/`; only the realistic adjudication is recorded here as canon.*

## Setup (2020)

Eunomia founded in **California**. Michael = permanent benevolent dictator, full legitimacy, ~40M citizens, ~$300B annual budget + $25B personal cash, free trade and good US relations. Static-tech world (see `../starting-scenario.md`). Governing philosophy: *laws mean things and are enforced; then make the laws sensible.*

## Setup parameters (resolved)

- **Budget:** ~$300B/yr recurring state budget + **$25B personal**, the latter primarily invested into tech equities/industry (SpaceX, computer manufacturers, etc.).
- **Sovereignty:** **FULL** , Eunomia can override federal law (environmental review, immigration, criminal law, its own courts).
- **Immigration:** **controlled**. Tourism fairly open; strict early requirements; no entry or citizenship for people with criminal histories; strictly enforced borders/citizenship; primarily skills-based (hired-for-skills) inflow. Early focus on social culture, high trust, and keeping out cheaters/damagers. "No ceiling, civilized floor."
- **10 strikes:** ten strikes before the maximum penalty (e.g., ~20 yrs for a 10-time shoplifter). Prisons humane-but-austere (books, time, optional work; tiered rewards up to games and some remote work at best-behavior levels). Rehabilitation-oriented, certain escalation.
- **Dictatorship:** retained for now; succession deferred.

## 2020-2025 , CANON (realistic adjudication)

Full sovereignty is the decisive variable: it deletes the pessimistic run's biggest wall (federal law). What's left limiting Michael is physics and time, not lawyers.

- **Abundance / housing , strong.** No NEPA, no federal courts, no veto-by-committee: the red-tape strike team genuinely works, permitting becomes fast and rules-based, and tiered build-quality bonuses pull in builders. The binding constraint is now physical , in a frozen-tech world, construction labor and materials cap the pace , so it's a real boom, not magic. Over five years housing supply expands substantially, prices soften, and **homelessness's decades-long rise reverses into a clear decline** (not eliminated in five years, but the trajectory is broken). HSR breaks ground and lays real track. Biggest QALY mover (housing security is a top wellbeing lever).
- **Criminal justice , good, still maturing.** Strict certain enforcement + the published 10-strikes ladder + humane-but-austere, rehabilitation-oriented prisons produce a moderate crime decline and a clear *fairness* gain. Violent crime and recidivism trend down as treatment and reentry scale; property crime is stickier early (ten chances means persistent petty offenders take longer to deter), but certain escalation bites by 2025. Incarceration-harm falls (humane prisons, smaller better-targeted population) , a real QALY gain for the bottom. The deep recidivism payoff is still compounding; five years is early.
- **Immigration & social trust , strong.** Controlled, skills-based, criminal-free, strictly-enforced borders build exactly what was intended: rising social trust, low crime, a high-skill talent influx, and a cohering build-and-ambition culture with a civilized floor. Honest tradeoff: controlled inflow grows the population slowly, which caps **total**-QALY scaling even as it lifts **average/median** quality and trust.
- **Economy & the $25B tech bet , solid but indirect.** Strict rules + aligned incentives + the talent influx make the economy strong and clean. The personal $25B into SpaceX/computer-makers/etc. compounds financially and anchors an advanced-industry base , but honest caveat: in a **frozen-tech** world there are no breakthroughs to capture, so the upside is capped and the payoff is long-game economic strength, not a 2020-2025 QALY mover. It builds future fiscal capacity more than present wellbeing.
- **Order & governance , credible.** Hardened fare gates restore transit order and, delivered competently with the honest "cheaper-then-free, but rules are real" message, land as the emblem of the project rather than petty cruelty. The Yudkowsky pyramid is bedding in (channeling local input on housing/town issues, building legitimacy), though five years is too short to prove it as a durable institution, and it still rests on Michael's personal competence , the deferred succession question is the main long-run risk to durability.

**QALY read at 2025 (directional, not final):**
- **Absolute:** already-high California QALYs tick up solidly (housing, crime, trust, order).
- **Diff-from-expected:** clearly positive , California's real-world path was stagnation/decline on exactly these fronts, so the counterfactual gain is real and growing.
- **Median:** lifting (homelessness down, crime down, humane justice, high floor) , a genuine bottom-raise.
- **Total:** growing modestly (controlled immigration = slower population growth).
- **World:** Eunomia is becoming a visible, credible model (sovereign Abundance + enforced-but-fair governance); spillover is just beginning, but the demonstration value is real for the world points.

**Net:** a strong, leaning-optimistic first five years , full sovereignty resolved the biggest risk in Michael's favor and the policies are well-designed , with honest limits from frozen-tech build capacity, the indirect tech bet, early property-crime stickiness, and the unproven long-term durability of the pyramid/succession.
