**Read:** Eunomia owns the per-capita *level* (absolute avg + median) and ties the early/contestable points; Bangladesh owns *scale* (total both ways) and the *bottom-lift* (median diff). The corruption drag plus Bangladesh's already-improving baseline neutralize Bangladesh's average-diff edge, keeping the leg close.

**Structural note:** the board rewards scale fairly hard (total x2 + world x3 = 5 of 9 points tilt toward large, copyable nations). Open to rebalancing if a structural scale-advantage feels off (e.g., per-capita-weight the total, trim world to 1 point, or add weight to absolute level).
