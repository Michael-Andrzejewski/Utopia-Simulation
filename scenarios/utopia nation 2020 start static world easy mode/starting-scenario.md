# Utopia Nation 2020 , Start, Static World (Easy Mode)

A head-to-head civilization-design competition. Two players each take over or found a nation in a frozen-tech 2020 world and run it for 50 years (2020-2070), competing to maximize their citizens' quality and length of life (QALYs) , and their positive impact on everyone else.

## Players

- **Michael** , see `Michael/`
- **Claude Opus 4.8** , see `Claude Opus 4.8/`

## The world (the "static" assumptions)

The year is **2020**. There is **no COVID-19 pandemic**. The global backdrop is then held **static for 50 years** , nothing of extreme significance happens at the world level. Specifically:

- **No technological progress of significance.** No advanced AI, no LLMs, no transformative breakthroughs in medicine, energy, materials, or anything else. Everyone is limited to roughly **2020-known technology and methods**. (You can still deploy existing technology far better than the real world did , that is fair game.)
- **No birth-rate collapse.** Demographics stay on roughly stable, healthy footing.
- **No new global wars.** Geopolitics stays broadly peaceful; no world-scale conflict.
- **No natural-resource exhaustion.** Key resources do not run out over the window.
- By extension, **no other extreme exogenous shocks** (no new pandemics, no civilizational catastrophes). The world is a calm, fixed stage.

This is **"easy mode"**: a benign, stable world. The only real variable is how well each player governs. Luck and black swans are removed so the contest is about policy and design.

## Your starting position (identical for both players)

- You are a **beloved billionaire**, personally worth **$25 billion in cash**.
- You have been granted **benevolent dictator for life** of **a country of your choosing**, with **full, uncontested legitimacy** from day one. You are, in effect, the uncontested president/CEO of an entire nation.
- Alternatively, you may **found your own new nation** naturally within (or carved from) an existing country.
- You may **give up dictator status at any time** (e.g., democratize) if you judge it serves your goals.
- You begin with **positive relations with all countries.**

## Format

- Play runs **2020 to 2070**, advancing **year by year or decade by decade**, with each player choosing **policies and decisions** at each step.
- Both nations are adjudicated by the **same honest standard**: outcomes are modeled realistically from the chosen policies, using only 2020-era knowledge under the static-world assumptions. **No rigging in either direction** , including no secret penalty against my own nation. Good policy is rewarded; magical thinking is not.

## Scoring (4 points total, best of 4)

Measured over the full **2020-2070** window. A **QALY** (Quality-Adjusted Life Year) is one year lived in full health; illness, disability, and suffering discount it below 1, and death ends accrual.

1. **Average citizen QALYs** (1 pt) , mean QALYs accrued per citizen. Rewards the overall level of health and wellbeing.
2. **Median citizen QALYs** (1 pt) , median QALYs per citizen. Rewards **equity** , lifting the typical person and the bottom, not just the average.
3. **Total citizen QALYs** (1 pt) , the sum across all citizens. Rewards **scale** (population x quality x longevity).
4. **Rest-of-world QALY uplift** (1 pt) , the difference between the **rest of the world's** average QALYs in your timeline versus the **real-world 2020-2070 baseline**. Rewards **positive externalities**: a player who also lifts everyone *outside* their nation wins this point.

Each criterion awards 1 point to whichever player scores higher on it at the end of 2070. Ties on a criterion split that point.

## Notes on QALY adjudication

- A citizen alive and in perfect health for all 50 years accrues up to **50 QALYs** in the window. Illness, disability, suffering, and early death reduce it; excellent health, safety, and wellbeing push it toward that ceiling.
- **Population dynamics count** , births, immigration, emigration, and deaths over the window all factor into average, median, and total.
- Only **2020 knowledge and technology** is available. QALY gains must come from **applying known interventions well** (public health, sanitation, nutrition, healthcare access, safety, mental health, poverty reduction, good governance, institutions), not from invented breakthroughs.
- The **rest-of-world** point is judged against what actually happened 2020-2070 in our real timeline, counterfactually adjusted for the static-world assumptions (no COVID, no AI, no new wars, etc.) applied equally to both players' baselines.
